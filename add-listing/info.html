Terms of Adding a Ministry

Adding a Church, Choir or Band means you must be a bonefide member of the ministry.

The user adding a ministry would be in charge of the ministry's account, and would as well be sent a confirmation email by Wakachurch's admin in other to authenticate the account.

If the user who added the ministry is being confirmed by wakachurch's admin is false, this will lead to the removal of the ministry's account on the user's profile.

A user can only add a Church, Choir and a band in as much as the user is an active member of the three ministries.

Once a Ministry is added and authenticated no other user can add the ministry again

Ministries added by wakachurch's admin would be released if the ministry is claimed by the owner or leader of the said ministry and confirmed by us.
